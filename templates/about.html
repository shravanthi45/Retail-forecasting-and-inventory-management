The Retail Forecasting and Inventory Management System helps retail businesses analyze their sales data and predict future demand using data-driven techniques.

The system processes historical sales records to generate accurate demand forecasts, analyze stock levels, and support better inventory decisions.

It is built using Python, Flask, Pandas, and Plotly to provide a modern, interactive and efficient retail analytics platform.


📈 Sales Analysis

Visualizes sales trends, monthly growth, and product performance using interactive charts.

📦 Inventory Control

Calculates stock levels, reorder points, and helps prevent overstocking and stock-outs.

🔮 Demand Forecasting

Uses historical sales data to predict future demand and improve planning accuracy.


🎯 Project Objectives

  • Improve demand prediction accuracy
  • Optimize inventory management
  • Reduce business losses due to overstock or shortage
  • Support data-driven retail decisions
